# Delegation: Specialists vs Minions

**Date:** 2026-04-11 (S35)

## Learning

Lean-delegation (what+why only, no how) applies to **specialist agents** — Katarina, Fiora, Syndra, Swain, Pyke, Bard, Lissandra, Shen, etc. They have skills, docs, and domain judgment. Over-specifying wastes their expertise and violates the lean-delegation rule.

**Minions are the exception.** Yuumi and Skarner are stateless, domain-agnostic errand runners. They do not fill in gaps from sparse instructions. Give them explicit, detailed task descriptions — what files, what changes, what format, what to commit.

## Trigger

Caught in real time: I gave Katarina 7 numbered steps with explanations for an audit execution task instead of pointing her at the audit report and stepping back. Duong flagged it immediately.

## Rule of thumb

- Specialist → "Here's the task and why. You know how."
- Minion → "Here's exactly what to do, step by step."
